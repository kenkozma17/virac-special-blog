Nestled in the heart of Gogon Sirangan, Dakila on the ground floor of a bustling narrow street exudes a unique charm reminiscent of the neighborhoods one might find in SOHO, New York. With its modern, cast iron facades, this café draws a diverse crowd, catering to corporate professionals, academics, and artists alike, providing a space for both work and relaxation.

The ambiance at Dakila is dark and cool, creating an atmosphere that fosters focus and creativity, making it the perfect spot for solo laptop sessions or casual meetups with colleagues. But what truly sets Dakila apart is its commitment to quality coffee and an ever-evolving menu of delectable pastries and meals.

Their Iced Coffee creations are nothing short of sensational, offering a variety of options that seamlessly blend invigoration and refreshment with a rich coffee flavor that beckons coffee enthusiasts. Each sip is an enticing journey of curiosity and delight, perfectly complemented by a selection of mouthwatering treats like banana toffee pie, cheesecake, and flavored muffins.

As the warmer weather arrives, Dakila introduces Rice Bowl meals at an affordable
P89, enticing a diverse crowd eager to savor these economically friendly and satisfying
dishes. Choices include fried shrimp, pork lumpia, or wonton, each creating a symphony
of flavors that captivate the palate. And if you're in the mood for pasta, Dakila's
Alfredo, a top choice as of August, promises an exquisite dining experience.

Dakila is not just a café; it's a hub for artistic expression and cultural fusion. The establishment proudly supports local musical artists like Ms. Mei Teves and even more singer-songwriters, hosting special evenings dedicated to their talents. This creative infusion adds a new cultural dimension to Virac, showcasing the town on a global stage.

The Dakila Family is committed to offering exceptional food and genuine customer service. They open their doors from 10 am to 9 pm every Tuesday to Sunday, with Mondays reserved for a much-deserved break. Dakila is more than a café; it's a destination where industrial chic, artistry, coffee, and community come together to create an experience that is truly remarkable.
